How to remove "alamy" watermarks. Free guide for you boys:

Find the image id. Should be at the bottom right corner.

Now you paste the id on a link like this: https: //c7.alamy.com/zooms/15/(imageid)/(image id).jpg

Eample:
My id: CPJ1EG
Result: https://c7.alamy.com/zooms/15/CPJ1EG/CPJ1EG.jpg
